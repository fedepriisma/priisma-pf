# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

People who already get it: founders, product people, fellow builders looking
for a partner in crime. They arrive via word of mouth or Showbliz, skim in
under a minute, and either recognize the work or bounce. Explicitly NOT for
procurement-style clients, "corporate assholes," or anyone who needs to be
sold to; bouncing them is a feature.

## Product Purpose

Fede Bozo's one-page personal site (priisma.com). Not a portfolio, not a
resume: a beacon and a filter. Success = the right person emails
federico@priisma.com; the wrong person leaves. Design IS the product.

## Positioning

I design by building: head-of-design caliber polymath who ships whole
products solo with AI. Showbliz is the proof, not a promise.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: email federico@priisma.com. Secondary: visit showbliz.com.
- The line a visitor remembers: "I design by building."
- Belief ladder: (1) this person ships real things, (2) alone, fast, with
  AI, (3) at head-of-design quality, (4) I want them on my side.
- Proof on hand: Showbliz itself (live, 40+ venues, bilingual, built solo in
  3 months) with its live feed on the page. No testimonials possible
  (confidentiality). Honest numbers only; never borrow metrics (the $1.5M
  figure is airTRFX/EveryMundo's, not Cigarwell's). "IRU" must never appear.

## Brand Personality

Direct, sharp, warm. First person "I", never studio "we". Kind, fun, loves
language; swears when it lands; zero hedging. Simplicity over persuasion:
"no more over-explanation." Copy must never read as a junior building
credibility; Fede is done proving himself.

## Anti-references

- Generic agency/SaaS portfolio templates; hero-metric layouts.
- Persuasion-shaped copy ("we help brands unlock...").
- Engagement-tier menus, lead-gen quizzes, testimonial walls.
- Rejected aesthetic directions (do NOT return): paper+yellow+Cabinet
  Grotesk; bone+flame+Fraunces; dark+teal+Albert Sans/Geist.

## Design Principles

1. Beacon, not brochure: attract the right people, filter the rest.
2. Show, don't sell: the live Showbliz feed is the argument.
3. One page, no over-explanation: old work is a numbered archive list
   (Cigarwell, QuotingBill, GoDark, airTRFX, MCH Anywhere), never case
   studies.
4. Honest numbers only.
5. Spec-sheet discipline: the page reads like a printed technical data sheet
   with one hot accent (see DESIGN.md).

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. `--accent-text` token exists because raw coral fails AA at small
sizes on paper. Visible focus outlines, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected
(marquee pausable), padded ticker repeats aria-hidden so screen readers hear
each show once.
